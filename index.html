About Me

Precious Moments Photography

My name is Tanya Selina Akter and I am the photographer and owner of Precious Moments Photography. I live in the fun state of Wisconsin, but will travel to wherever my photography takes me.
Something that you might be interested to know about me, before you call me and hear my accent is that I am originally from Ukraine, born and raised. In Fall of 2006 I met my wonderful husband who I fell in love with and married in 2007 in Kiev, Ukraine (he was a missionary there for 2 years).
I thank God for him, he is the best, always loves and supports me no matter what! Now we have 3 beautiful children; a girl named Ksenia Pearl who is 6- years-old, a 4-year-old son named Sky and a new baby named Eli James, they are my everything. They really are my inspiration and I love them with all my heart!
Anyway, there is much more to tell, but I just want to share the main things in my life that are good. Photography is one of those best things! I am always looking for new and better ways to make pictures unique and more wonderful for my customers. I have had so many repeat customers since my studio opened in 2009. My customers have always been amazing and kind to me.

I hope I will get the chance to meet you and share my passion by capturing the precious moments of YOUR LIFE! Hope to meet you soon.
